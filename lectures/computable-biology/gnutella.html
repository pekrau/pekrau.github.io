Bioinformatics à la Gnutella

A project to investigate data sharing through a mechanism inspired by Gnutella, FreeNet, Napster: A decentralised, peer-to-peer system.

Examples of applications:

  • Distributed annotation of data
  • Small experimental communities for data modelling
  • Sharing of data among collaborating groups

Specifications and protocols for:

  • Many independent nodes (data sites)
  • Search and download of datasets
  • Non-anonymous, traceable, authorship
  • Scaleability
  • Standard components: XML, SOAP, Corba...
