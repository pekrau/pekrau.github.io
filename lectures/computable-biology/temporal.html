Temporal bioinformatics

A research programme:

Formalizing the description of biological processes

  • Linear processes; development, cascades
  • Circular (looping) processes (cell cycle, circadian rythm)
  • Conditional processes, probabilities
  • Perturbations
  • Relationship phenomenon vs. mechanism
  • Sequential/parallel, synchronous/asynchronous
  • Different levels of abstractions (macroscopic, molecular)
  • Hierarchic descriptions natural to a biologist: cell cycle in yeast vs. mammals
