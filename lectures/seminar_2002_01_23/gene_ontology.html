Gene Ontology http://www.geneontology.org


Controlled vocabulary ("GO terms") to describe genes and gene products.


Three hierarchies of terms (directed acyclic graphs).

  • Molecular function
    Tasks performed by individual gene products; chemistry

  • Biological process
    Broad biological goals accomplished by ordered assemblies of molecular functions

  • Cellular component
    Subcellular structures, locations, macromolecular complexes


Association: Connect GO terms with a gene or gene product, based on evidence (references) of some kind.

Produced by curators at different database sites (SGD, TAIR, Flybase, MGI, Wormbase,... but not Ensembl!).
