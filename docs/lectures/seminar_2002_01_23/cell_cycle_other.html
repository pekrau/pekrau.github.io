Examples of other properties that could be included in the map

    • bud
    • spindle

  • Duplication and organisational changes of the chromosomes.
  • Transcription factor levels.
  • Transcriptional activity at given chromosomal locations.
  • Flux through given pathways.
  • Microscopy images; fluorescence imaging.
  • Changes of mRNA levels of uncharacterized genes.
