Systems Biology


Formulation, simulation and analysis of biological systems using mathematical models.


Goal

  • Understand macroscopic biological phenomena in terms of molecular mechanisms.
  • Connect physiology and cell biology with molecular mechanisms in a stricter way.
  • Discover the organizing principles underlying biochemical networks.


Requirements

  • Detailed descriptions of molecular mechanisms (reaction pathways)
  • High-resolution data sets (functional genomics experiments)


Utility

  • Perturbation analysis
  • Hypotheses for roles of uncharacterized genes
  • Analysis of robustness in a system
  • Prediction of intervention points (medicine)
