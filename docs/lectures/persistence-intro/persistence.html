What is persistence?

  • Data is persistent when stored on a permanent medium.
    • Must survive program termination.
    • Must survive computer shutdown.

  • The data may be read by independently executing programs.

  • The data may be added/updated by independently executing programs; coordination required.

  • Manydifferent kinds of technology
    • Hard disks (most common today)
    • Optical CD's
    • Floppy disk
    • Magnetic tape
    • Other technologies exists and new will probably be invented.
